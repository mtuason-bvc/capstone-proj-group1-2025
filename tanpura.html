Used in Indian classical music, Tanpura is a stringed instrument that provides a harmonic drone with resonant intervals that agitate a wide range of hormonics. In modern times, an electronic box serves this function.

Use this tool to play the main turning pitch of Bb or A, and perfect 4ths or 5ths to check your turning
